Privacy Policy – Nexus Sales CRM Extension

Privacy Policy – Nexus Sales CRM Extension Privacy Policy Last updated: December 29, 2025 Overview The Nexus Sales CRM browser extension (“the Extension”) is designed to allow users to transfer form data into their Nexus Sales CRM account. We take user privacy seriously and are committed to protecting any information processed by the Extension. Information the Extension Collects The Extension may collect the following information only when the user explicitly activates it: Form field data visible on the current webpage (e.g., name, email, phone, address) Workflow selection made by the user Nexus Sales CRM API key entered by the user The Extension does not automatically scrape data. Data is collected only when the user clicks the transfer button. How Information Is Used Collected data is used solely to: Transfer user-selected form data into the user’s Nexus Sales CRM account Associate the data with a selected workflow inside Nexus Sales CRM The Extension does not: Sell data Share data with third parties Use data for advertising or tracking Store data beyond the immediate transfer operation API Key Handling The API key is provided directly by the user. The API key is used only to authenticate requests to Nexus Sales CRM. The API key is stored locally in the browser (if the user chooses “Save as default”) and is never transmitted to third-party services. Data Storage No scraped form data is permanently stored by the Extension. Data exists only temporarily in memory for the purpose of transferring it to Nexus Sales CRM. No analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts are used. Permissions Explanation The Extension requests access to webpage content only to read form fields selected by the user. It does not monitor browsing activity, keystrokes, or pages unrelated to user actions. Third-Party Services The Extension communicates only with: Nexus Sales CRM servers, as explicitly initiated by the user No other third-party services are contacted. Children’s Privacy The Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Contact If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the Extension, you may contact: Nexus Sales CRM Email: support@nexussales.io Website: https://nexussales.io
